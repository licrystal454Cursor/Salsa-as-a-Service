## Kitchen Management Systems

### Kitchen Display Systems (KDS)

Integrate KDS platforms to keep kitchen execution, prep planning, and inventory in sync.

**Data Flow:**
```
Salsa ‚Üí SAP: Purchase orders, inventory movements, production data
SAP ‚Üí Salsa: Master data, cost centers, GL accounts, suppliers
```

This flow ensures order changes, prep status, and inventory updates stay synchronized across POS, Salsa, and kitchen systems.
